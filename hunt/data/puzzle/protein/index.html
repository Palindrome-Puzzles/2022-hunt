Notes on pdb files:

  • The molecular viewing program RCSB 3D View (web app, www.rcsb.org/3d-view) is recommended for this.
    • Jena3D (web app, jena3d.leibniz-fli.de/) and ChimeraX (downloadable program, www.cgl.ucsf.edu/chimerax/download.html) have additional features you may find useful.
  • The files are also viewable in any standard text editor.
  • The atom X represents N/O, C6N2O, or an unknown side chain, as appropriate.
