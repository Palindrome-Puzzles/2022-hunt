The initial letters of the surnames of the Steves (in clue order) spell out HE PLAYED VASQUEZ IN THAT BULLOCK FILM.

The film in question is All About Steve, in which Sandra Bullock played a crossword constructor who wrote a crossword in which every single clue referenced Steve, a guy she had fallen in love with (Ms. Bullock won a Golden Raspberry for her performance).

The answer is the actor who played the role of Vasquez in All About Steve: JASON JONES.
