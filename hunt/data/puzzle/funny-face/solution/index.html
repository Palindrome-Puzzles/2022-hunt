Each of the 26 faces is a composite of the faces of two actors who have played the same character based on a book in a movie or tv series. The faces are in alphabetical order by character name. The character enumerations at the bottom are simply in order of increasing length; they must be matched to the correct face. No actor or book source is used more than once (there are multiple marvel comic book characters created by Stan Lee, but each from a different franchise).

The 2 numbers below each face indicate extraction indexes into the character name; the left index extracts an ordering letter A through Z, and the right index extracts the clue phrase letter. Placed in order of first index A > Z, the second set of letters spells out JOE HARDY IN DAMN YANKEES MOVIE. Joe Hardy was the only major role in the Damn Yankees film that had not been played by the same actor in the Broadway play; Tab Hunter replaced Stephen Douglass in that role. The answer is TAB HUNTER.
