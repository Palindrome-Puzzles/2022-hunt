Authors' Notes

Most of my ideas for puzzles are not inspired by a particular answer.

This time, we needed a puzzle for the answer MAKE A MOVE, in a round where all the puzzles would be themed on rodents. Through the Shrek song, this led me to the Three Blind Mice, which led me to write a puzzle about chopping off the tails (an established wordplay action).

After that, it was just fun to come up with clues, and a word list that was in puzzle order and already in alphabetical order.
