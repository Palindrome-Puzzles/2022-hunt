The alien symbols can be mapped to lowercase letters as follows:

  • Ascending letters are represented by the large caret, either with a circle (for b and d), a crossbar (for f and t), or unadorned (for h, k, and l).
  • Descending letters are represented by the v-shape, either with a circle (for g, p and q), or unadorned (for j and y).
  • Letters that neither ascend not descend are represented by the equals sign, each modified to indicate the symmetry of that letter. A horizontal line for c; a vertical line for v and w; an x (no symmetry) for m, n, and r; a circle of arrows for s and z; and a circle of arrows and two lines for x.
  • Vowels are represented by three dots.
