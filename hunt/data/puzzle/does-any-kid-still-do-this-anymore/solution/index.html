Each line of trigrams decodes to a Bart Simpson chalkboard gag with one of the words replaced with another word or words. The puzzle title is itself a chalkboard gag.

Sort the chalkboard gags by episode number. The first letters of the correct words from the gags spell out the clue phrase: START TOUR AT COLMA THEN USE TITLES

Looking up the replacement words will lead you to discover that they are BART stations (Bay Area Rapid Transit), and the clue phrase confirms it.

Using https://www.bart.gov/system-map and starting at Colma, travel to each station in order (Colma ➡ Glen Park, Glen Park ➡ Sixteenth St Mission, etc.), counting the stations in each trip.

Index the number of stations into The Simpsons episode title that the chalkboard gag came from to reveal the answer: MAKE A STAR TREK EPISODE ABOUT HUNT.
