Author's Note

I've wanted to write a pure Eurovision puzzle for Hunt for Y E A R S. It's a 65-year dataset with plenty of angles for puzzle-writing.

Once we decided that we wanted teams to write Eurovision pastiches as part of the Self-Help tasks, I knew I wanted to make sure teams had seen "Love Love Peace Peace" so they knew what that should look like:

Once that was the starting point, the rest of the puzzle came together quickly, pulling in as many of the songs LLPP references as I could.
