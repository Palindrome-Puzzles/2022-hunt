The two investigators hosting the Nick-and-Nora-style dinner party are the chimpanzee scientists Cornelius and Zira from the original Planet of the Apes. Each of their guests is a fictional ape (orangutan, gorilla, bonobo, or chimpanzee) or a human being played by Charlton Heston in a film. (Bright Eyes is what Zira calls Charlton Heston's character in Planet of the Apes, and so she uses the same nickname for the similar-looking human beings here.)

Each guest has committed a crime against some other guest; the bolded words in the accusation clue a word that is a transaddition of the victim's name. The accusation also clues who the perpetrator is; to help in identification, the accusations are in alphabetical order by perpetrator.

In that order we have (using orange, yellow, green, cyan, and blue for orangutans, gorillas, human beings, bonobos, and chimpanzees):
