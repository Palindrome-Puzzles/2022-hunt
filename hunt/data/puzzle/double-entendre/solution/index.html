One is a transdeletion (2) of the contranym, so we can get a bigram that was removed from each contranym (in ORIGINAL there is the ambiguity as both IG and GI are present in the contranym, but should be easily disambiguated when placed next to other bigrams in the next step).

The other can be paired with an ordinal word to form a common phrase (e.g. LADY -> FIRST LADY). Order by the ordinal word (1st - 7th) and take the removed bigrams to get the final answer TODAY IS TONIGHT.
