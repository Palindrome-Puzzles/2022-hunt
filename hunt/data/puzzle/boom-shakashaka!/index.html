Nikolee came up with an interesting way to decorate this chocolate cake with some vanilla icing, and she hid her plans in two of her favorite puzzles - minesweeper and shakashaka! What else has she hidden underneath all that icing?

Number clues are either for minesweeper or for shakashaka. The cells that represent mines in minesweeper are the same as the cells that represent solid dark squares in shakashaka. One cell completely covered with vanilla icing (no mines, no triangles) is given.
