The acrostic from the clued answers spells NTH PLACE VERSUS A COMMON RIVAL.

Each shaded group in the grid is the list of people who came in a particular ordinal place (e.g. 2nd place) against one particular returning contestant, across all of the appearances of that contestant.

(Some of the people in the grid also appeared in multiple seasons, but getting the same ordinal number for an entire group makes the solution unique.)

The groups in the grid are given so that these returning contestants are in alphabetical order (by first name).

Author's Note

Where is BOSTON ROB, you ask? I wanted to fit this Survivor icon into the puzzle somewhere, ideally as one of the returning contestants. You would have used the name BOSTON ROB, and indexed and extracted from that, because that is unequivocally how he's known on the show. Unfortunately, this was too confusing to playtesters who weren't familiar enough with the show, and were relying on online lists that don't list people under nicknames, even one as prominent as that.
