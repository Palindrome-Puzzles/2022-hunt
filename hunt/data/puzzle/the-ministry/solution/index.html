This meta puzzle uses all 25 feeder puzzle answers in addition to the 5 Minister meta answers.

The flavor text mentions a mural outside of Hayden. This mural is the mural of engineers describing an elephant, and is pictured below.

This allows you to sort the five libraries from left to right based on the locations of the Ministers.

  • BARKER - SOMEWHAT BUGGY
  • LEWIS - MULTIPART COMPOSITION
  • ROTCH - EVEN IN WIDTH
  • DEWEY - COLORFUL HEAD
  • HAYDEN - GOES ON LONGER THAN WAR AND PEACE

Each of these answers can be interpreted as a condition that the answers may or may not have.

  • BARKER - SOMEWHAT BUGGY (contains the name of a bug)
  • LEWIS - MULTIPART COMPOSITION (has more than 1 word)
  • ROTCH - EVEN IN WIDTH (has an even number of letters)
  • DEWEY - COLORFUL HEAD (begins with ROYGBIV)
  • HAYDEN - GOES ON LONGER THAN WAR AND PEACE (has more than 11 letters)
