Another meteorite has landed near the blasted heath west of Arkham. In this meteorite, just as in the one that had landed on the Gardner farm years before, a large colored globule was found embedded. This time after it burst, strangely colored yet hauntingly familiar inscriptions were found on the walls of the cavity the globule had occupied.

Dispatched from Miskatonic University, Professor Upham peers at those inscriptions and struggles to make sense of them. He marvels at the fact that they seem to be written in the Latin alphabet and that they take the form of long division arithmetic problems. He starts to work through the logic and makes several important deductions. But just as he is about to complete a chain of observations and deductions that would allow him to identify the value of C (and as an immediate corollary, the value of H), he succumbs to incurable madness, leaving it to you to complete his work…

As it does, you hear the cultists of Cthulhu start to chant as they don their ceremonial garb. In a sign of your own impending madness, you experience a sort of synesthesia and can actually see their words as they speak, some of which are imbued with those colors out of space.
